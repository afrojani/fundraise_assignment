Our Donate Plan

There are lots of ways you can fundraise – through your work, school, university or individually. To help get you started, we’ve included some fun ideas and resources on this page, and our dedicated fundraising team are here to support you.

Food for Children

Donate non perishable food for the street children. Your donation can help a hungry children. No child deserves to go to bed in hungry stomach."food for all" is our motto.

Shelter for Children

Donate money to build shelter for street children. Thousands of children sleep under the open sky. your donation can help to provide them a safe shelter.

Education for Children

Education is a fundamental need of a child. Every child deserves to go to school. Your donation can help a child to grow as a educated person.
